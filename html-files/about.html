Our Story

We believe everyday moments should be beautiful

Founded by interior designer Jack Davidson and named in honour of his beloved first dog, Jack & Abel curates artisan-made pieces that balance elegance with functionality; quality with integrity.

From elegant homeware to practical essentials, our collections – each chosen with Jack’s discerning eye – celebrate craftsmanship and thoughtful design. Whether you’re seeking the perfect addition to your kitchen table or a gift to be treasured, Jack & Abel’s thoughtful and lovely pieces bring both beauty and purpose into your home.

At the heart of our brand is a deep respect for both people and planet. We champion ethical practises, working with talented independent makers who share our dedication to sustainability and quality. By sharing the stories behind their craftsmanship, we nurture a connection between you and the hands that bring these pieces to life.

Welcome to Jack & Abel, where we warmly invite you to Live in Style.

Meet Our Team

Jack Davidson

As the founder of his eponymous London-based interior design studio, Jack Davidson Interior Design, Jack has a keen eye for pieces that are both beautiful and functional. While sourcing for his projects, he was inspired to create Jack & Abel, a curated collection of timeless homeware and accessories that reflects his passion for craftsmanship and considered design.

Jack specialises in creating elegant, characterful spaces that honour a building’s heritage while seamlessly accommodating modern life. This philosophy guides the selection at Jack & Abel, where our commitment to sustainability and artisan craftsmanship ensures every piece embodies enduring style and integrity.

Nikki Willemen

Nikki’s background as an interior designer was shaped by her experience working under the acclaimed Jane Ormsby Gore at JR Design. During her time there, she refined her design expertise on prestigious projects, including the creation of Robin Birley’s 5 Hertford Street in Mayfair.

Nikki brings her impeccable eye for design, creativity, and meticulous organisational skills to Jack & Abel. She oversees all aspects of operations, from carefully sourcing stock and ensuring the seamless running of the website to leading our marketing and cultivating meaningful connections with our makers and customers.
